Since 1974 · three generations

Three generations. One standard.

Family-owned since 1974, when our founder turned a Taichung workshop into a factory. We make for the world’s brands — and never sell our own.

What began in 1974 as a single Taichung workshop grew, under our founder, into a factory built to make for others. The second generation carried that work into Europe, the Commonwealth and the Americas — always the maker behind the brand, never a rival to it. Today the third generation runs the business across Taiwan and China, serving 180+ brands and distributors in 37 markets. Three generations, one rule that has never changed: we make your products, and we never sell our own.
